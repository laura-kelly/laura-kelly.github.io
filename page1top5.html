My Top 5 Chocolate Shops in Paris

1. Chocolat Chapon

A quaint shop with lots of fun gift options (if you're there around the holidays, check out the advent calendars). My favorite chocolate is the dark chocolate with caramel and almonds, but you really can't go wrong with any of the options. What really sets this place apart from the others, however, is its chocolate mousse bar - it's simply the best!

   

2. Patrick Roger

This store may feel more like an art gallery than a chocolate shop at times - Chocolatier Patrick Roger prides himself on being not only a great chocolate maker but also a master chocolate sculpter. Here you'll find a mix of traditional chocolates and more unique flavors, such as lemon basil. My favorite is one of the less adventurous chocolates, the crunchy hazelnut praline.

   

3. Pierre Hermé

Best know for its macarons, but the chocolates are also worth a try. Or go for a chocolate macaron. One of their signature flavors is Mogador (milk chocolate and passion fruit), and it can be found in both chocolates and macarons.
